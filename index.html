Privacy Policy for ViroTap

Effective Date: July 27, 2025

We, the developers of ViroTap, respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you may provide while playing our game.

1. Data Collection

The game does not collect any personal information such as name, address, or email address.

2. Firebase

We use Firebase Realtime Database to store high scores and display leaderboards. This includes:

  • Nickname (entered by the player)

This data is shown publicly on the leaderboard and is not used for identifying individuals.

3. Audio and Multimedia

The game plays local sound effects but does not access the microphone, camera, or any other sensors.

ViroTap may show advertisements via services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect anonymized device data to deliver relevant ads.

5. Security

All data transferred via Firebase is secured and not shared with third parties.

6. Consent

By installing and using ViroTap, you agree to this privacy policy.
